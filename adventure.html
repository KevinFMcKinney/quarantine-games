RuneScape

RuneScape is a medieval fantasy massively multiplayer online role playing game. It is the largest and most regularly updated massively multiplayer online roleplaying game. There are no set goals in this game and users are free to pursue whatever objectives they desire using their own custom avatar. Download it here!

League of Legends

League of Legends is a free online multiplayer game where users battle against one another in teams of five. Each user chooses a champion who has specific abilities and serves a general role in the game. League of Legends is one of the most popular esports in the world and is the most played PC game in North America and Europe. Check it out here!
